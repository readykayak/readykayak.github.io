Kayaking the Welland Recreational Canal

Looking for a relaxing paddle in the Niagara region? The Welland Recreational Canal offers calm water, beautiful scenery and a welcoming environment for beginners, families and experienced paddlers alike. ReadyKayak delivers single and tandem kayaks to approved launch sites so you can spend more time enjoying the water.

Why Paddle the Welland Canal?

  • Calm, flat water ideal for beginners.
  • Perfect for families and casual paddlers.
  • Scenic views along the recreational canal.
  • Great choice for photography and wildlife viewing.
  • Free kayak delivery to approved launch sites.

What Is Included?

  • Single and tandem kayaks
  • Paddles
  • Life jackets
  • Basic safety instructions

How Your Rental Works

  1. Book your kayak online.
  2. Select a Welland launch location.
  3. We'll deliver your kayak.
  4. Enjoy your paddle on the canal.
  5. Return the kayak to the same launch location at the scheduled time.

Who Is This Route Best For?

The Welland Recreational Canal is an excellent choice for first-time kayakers, families, couples and anyone looking for a peaceful paddle away from strong currents.
