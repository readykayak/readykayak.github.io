Queenston to Niagara-on-the-Lake Kayak Adventure

Discover one of the Niagara region's most memorable paddling experiences. This scenic one-way route begins in Queenston and finishes near Mississauga Beach in historic Niagara-on-the-Lake.

Why You'll Love This Trip

  • Beautiful river scenery throughout the journey.
  • A unique one-way paddle ending in Old Town Niagara-on-the-Lake.
  • Great for couples, friends and visitors exploring Niagara.
  • Free kayak delivery to the launch site.

How It Works

  1. Book your kayak online.
  2. Meet us at the Queenston launch site.
  3. We provide your kayak, paddle and life jacket.
  4. Paddle downstream to Niagara-on-the-Lake.
  5. Finish near Mississauga Beach at the scheduled pickup location.

What's Included

  • Single or tandem kayak
  • Paddles
  • Life jackets
  • Free delivery and pickup for this route
  • Safety instructions before departure

Who Is This Trip Best For?

This route is best suited to paddlers who are comfortable spending an extended period on the water. Always check weather and river conditions before your trip and follow all safety guidance provided before launching.
