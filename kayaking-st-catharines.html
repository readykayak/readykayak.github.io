Kayaking in St. Catharines

Looking for a peaceful kayaking experience in St. Catharines? ReadyKayak delivers single and tandem kayaks to approved launch sites, making it easy to enjoy a relaxing paddle close to the city.

A Great Place to Paddle

The waters around Henley Island and nearby sheltered areas offer a pleasant setting for recreational kayaking. On calm days, they're ideal for enjoying nature, spending time with family or friends, and building confidence if you're new to kayaking.

Why Choose ReadyKayak?

  • Free delivery to approved launch sites
  • Single and tandem kayaks available
  • Life jackets and paddles included
  • Easy online booking
  • Friendly service

How It Works

  1. Book your kayak online.
  2. Choose your approved St. Catharines launch location.
  3. We deliver your kayak.
  4. Enjoy your paddle.
  5. Return the kayak to the same launch location at the agreed time.

Who Is This Paddle Best For?

This area is a great choice for beginners, couples, families and anyone looking for a calm recreational paddle when weather and water conditions are suitable.
